About Me

Hi, I'm Natalie!

The heart behind Golden Paws & Company.

I'm a Purdue University graduate and currently work as a Program Manager at Adobe. I've called Colorado home for the past eight years and love taking advantage of everything it has to offer—whether that's exploring in the mountains with my husband and dogs or enjoying live music or comedy shows—I'm always on the move.

With over 10 years of pet-sitting experience through Rover and volunteer dog walking at Humane Colorado, I've had the joy of working with dogs of all breeds, ages, sizes, and personalities!

As a *slightly* obsessed dog mom to two golden retrievers, Zion and Maggie, I know how important it is to have someone you trust caring for your pup. Whether you're at work, traveling for the day, or just need an extra hand, my goal is simple: pet services, happy tails, and total peace of mind for you.
